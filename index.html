About

I'm a researcher and developer specializing in employing advanced computational techniques to analyze genetic data and unravel the evolutionary history of species and populations. My expertise lies in computational population genetics, mathematical modeling, and optimization algorithms.

Notably, I've led the development of GADMA, an easy-to-use software for automatic demographic inference.

Currently, my projects encompass a diverse array of topics, ranging from demographic inference and the inference of selection to conservation efforts for endangered species.
