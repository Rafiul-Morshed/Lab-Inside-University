About the Lab

Welcome to the Advanced Research Lab (ARL) at Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB). We focus on interdisciplinary research in robotics, AI, and space technology to solve real-world problems.

Founded in 2010, our lab collaborates with industry leaders and academic partners worldwide to push the boundaries of scientific discovery.
